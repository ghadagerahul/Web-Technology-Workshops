THIS IS THE BEST PLACES TO VISIT IN MAHARASTRA


RAIGAD

One of the most popular forts in Maharashtra, the Raigad Fort was also used as a prison during the Nizamshahi rule. It sits on the Sahyadri Mountain range and offers you the best views of the lush green valley below. Considered as one of the prides of the Marathas, the fort comes with a rich history. And 15 different names, each given to it by different rulers of Raigad.

PRATAPGAD

If you are heading to Satara, make sure you have enough time in your itinerary to visit the Pratapgad fort. Built in 1657, it offers you a breathtaking view of coastal Konkan and is one of the most popular forts in Maharashtra. SINHGAD

An ideal place to go trekking, the Sinhagad Fort is about 25 km away from Pune. It is 1312 m above sea level, and it is still pretty unclear as to when it was built. Some say that it is nearly 2000-years-old. The Battle of Sinhagad 1670, an important war, was fought from here. Apart from that, the fort has witnessed numerous battles through centuries. So if you wish to be taken back in time, visit Sinhagad fort.
